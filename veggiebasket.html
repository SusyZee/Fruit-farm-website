Our immense Tomato farm is of a total of 4 acres containing six different types of tomatoes. Broccoli are a very popular vegetable on our farm. Very high in nutrients, broccoli are highly requested during the autumn season. Eggplants on our farm are also a highly requested vegetable, especially with restaurants for making eggplant specialites with the Italian, Spanish and Greek cuisines. As for Carrots and Onions they are mainly used for sauces and are not at all costly on our farms. Potatoes instead, are a bit particular to pick on our farm, digging into the soil may not be pleasant for most so we have potatoes already picked for you.



Our farms are open 7 days a week and hours are from 8am-8pm. Come and discover how pleasing it may be to pick your own fruits and vegetables! We guarantee that it will also be of a lot of fun together with other families, and what a great way to make friends!!! Enjoy your stay, and meet new people. Come along with other friends and family and have a great time the whole day together!

Our farms are located along the Golden Horseshoe in Ontario, on the Queen Elizabeth Way directing towards Niagara Falls, precisely in a farmtown called Watertown, which is between Hamilton and Burlington, Ontario just a few kilometers away from Lake Ontario.

Our Green Veggies

Our bushel of green veggies consist of Broccoli, celery, various leafy greens, green sauerkrauts, cucumbers and zucchini.

Zucchini, an Italian delight!

Eggplant Baskets

Our two acre eggplant farm has two varieties of eggplants: the dark purple and the lighter striped purple. Eggplants are also available in a rounded version, and also an oblong shape. Eggplants are available for most of the year from April to October and are inexpensive and costs much less to directly pick the vegetables on our farm rather than buying in the supermarket which would be more or less three times more expensive. Usually people buy bushels of various vegetables and then putting them in a freezer ready to use during the winter, or put them in jars with olive oil, or vinegar which then can preserved for many months.

Costs only $4.99 per bushel

Carrot Baskets

Carrots are a very common everyday vegetable and used by almost everyone in everyday cooking and many different cultures. Carrots may also be pre-boiled and preserved in jars or is also used as a commonly frozen vegetable. Very versatile, very economic, carrots are one of the most commonly picked vegetable on our farm. Carrots are very rich in vitamin C, and filled with carotene, a very potential anti cancerous ingedient. Carrots are available even during some months in the winter, therefore making them one of the best foods for preservation all year long. Our carrots are organically grown, therefore free of pesticides and a very healthy vegetable. We have a limit of three bushels of carrots per family, since each bushel may weigh up to 30 pounds.

Costs only $5.99 per bushel

Broccoli Baskets

On our farm, broccoli picking is available during the colder months, since broccoli is considered a winter vegetable. We open un to broccoli season in October, and close during the first few weeks of December, depending on the weather and snowfall. Broccoli is another popular vegetable to preserve in the freezer and used as frozen food for the winter. Although broccoli is not a very economic vegetable, but is very high in nutrients and an excellent source of essential vitamins and minerals. There is no limit of purchase per bushel of broccoli, therefore you can pick as many as one would like.

Costs only $5.99 per bushel

Tomato Baskets

Tomatoes are also a very popular vegetable people pick on our farms. Tomatoes are used in every cuisine and in every culture and is widely used in everyday cooking. Tomatoes are used mainly for boiling and making sauces, therefore conserved in bottles or jars for prolonged use. Although many like tomatoes raw, they are quite popular in summer mixed with tuna and basil garnishings, which is a very fresh plate to eat in summer. Tomatoes are available in differnt sizes and shapes, such as round, oblong, pear shaped, etc. There are very many varieties of tomatoes, and we basically have the most common available on our farms.

Costs only $14.99 per bushel

Potato Baskets

Potatoes are a very versatile vegetable and also quite common in everyday cooking. Almost everyone is a potato lover, especially with children and potato chips, fried potatoes, roasted potatoes, etc. This vegetable is available on our farm, but not for picking. We prefer to do the work for you, as you must dig deep into the soil for the potatoes since they are grown underground. To avoid pickers arriving with shovels, bushels of already picked potatoes are available on our farm, and also ready for order.

Costs only $9.99 per bushel

Onion Baskets

Onions are important in Asian cuisine, but also widely used in the Southern Meditterrenean such as Italy, Spain and Greece. Onions can also be considered a spice, used for garnishing exuisite plates. Onions also have several types available, such as purple, gold, white, red and yellow. Just like potatoes, onions are to be picked underground, so we therefore already have onions available on our farm already picked. You may choose from all of the above types of onions available, we have them all on our farm, with no purchasing limit.

Costs only $5.99 per bushel

Baskets of Various Vegetables

We also offer bushels with an assortment of various vegetables, green or coloured, such as red and yellow peppers, zucchini, celery, tomatoes, onions and more. These bushels may be ordered online, and we also deliver to homes, to supermarkets and restaurants.

Price of $16.99 per bushel of 25lb

Bushels of not so common Vegetables

These types of bushels called "not so common vegetables" is because it contains a mixture of vegetables not commonly requested, such as fresh peas, beetroot, radishes, garlic and seasonings such as parsley. We actually consider this an interesting mixture of vegetables, but may not be very preferred.

Price of $16.99 per bushel of 20lb

Bushel of Common Everyday Vegetables

Last but not least, what we consider the most commonly used vegetables in everyday cooking, and that contain potatoes, onions, asparagus, brussel sprouts, peppers, carrots, broccoli, celery, cucumbers and zucchini. One of our most popular bushels and extremely requested from restaurants.

Price of $14.99 per bushel of 20lb
