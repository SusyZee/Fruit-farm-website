Assortment of Onions

We have a half acre piece of land full of our delicious golden onions. Onions are very commonly used for cooking Asian delights, and since are not expensive, our onions are available for only $1.09 per lb.

Also frequently used in Asian cooking, garlic is an essential ingrendient for the majority of their dishes. Garlic is also a very potential anti-cancer vegetable which should be consumed frequently. Also a very affordable vegetable, we offer garlic at a mere price of only 99 ₵ per lb.

The Scallion Onion instead is widely used in European cuisine and is part of the ever most famous "Meditterrenean diet", popular with the French and Spanish. We have an availability all year round also in abundance for only $1.29 per lb.

Assortment of Garnishings

Hot peppers are a specialty, and a "must" for certain cultures such as Mexican, Italian and Spanish. Mainly used in the cuisines of these three countries, hot peppers are a delight when used for garnishing and tomato sauces. Hot peppers are grown in our farm for only $2.09 per lb.

Olives are a medditerenean delight. produced mainly in the south of Europe, especially in Italy where their olive oil is very well appreciated, it is also common in Greece and Spain. Unfortunately only imported, costs may be quite elevated and range around the $20 per lb.

Celery is commonly used to flavour many dishes and sauces. Celery is also a good source of vitamin B, a very healthy vegetable, celery is not at all expensive and is grown almost anywhere. Prices are around $1.39 per lb.
